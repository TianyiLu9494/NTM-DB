Representative genomes: For species without a reference genome, one assembly of the type strain of the species is selected as representative

Type strain genome: are the archetypes of a species. Type strains form the backbone of prokaryotic systematics as nomenclatural types of species and subspecies, and comparisons with established type strains are mandatory when classifying novel strains (PMID: 19700448).
